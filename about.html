Doppelgänger | 2019

"You look like a Paul Gauguin painting"

          This project is an exploration of images from the Met Museum Collection in an attempt to find my art doppelgänger. I used machine learning to cluster images of portraits from the collection, and myself to see if I would be grouped near any Paul Gauguin paintings. Explore the project to see my results.
